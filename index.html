A houseplant is a plant that is grown indoors mainly for decorative purposes. There are some studies that have shown the benefits of owning some of these plants in your apartment or office. Let's find out why owning some of these green friends can be beneficial 🌱

Potential Benefits of Owning Houseplants

  1. 1. Allergy Relief

    Researchers found that rooms with plants have less dust and mold than rooms without any foliage. Leaves and other parts of the plants act as natural filters to catch allergens and other airborne particles.

  2. 2. Air Purifiers

    Extensive research by NASA has revealed that houseplants can remove up to 87 per cent of air toxin in 24 hours.

  3. 3. Mood Boosters

    Studies have also proven that indoor plants improve concentration and productivity (by up to 15 percent!), reduce stress levels and boost your mood. Keeping flowers and plants throughout the home and the workplace vastly increases happiness and lowers the likelihood of depression.

  4. 4. Increasing Humidity

    A study done by the Agricultural University of Norway, showed that houseplants, when grouped together, decrease the likelihood of dry skin, common colds, and sore throats.
